Strength training

Strength training, also known as weight training or resistance training, is a type of exercise that helps build muscle mass, strength, and endurance. It involves making your muscles contract against an outside resistance.

Deadlifts

The deadlift exercise is a relatively simple exercise to perform, a weight is lifted from a resting position on the floor to an upright position. The deadlift exercise utilizes multiple muscle groups to perform but has been used to strength the hips, thighs, and back musculature.

Zumba

Zumba is a fitness program that involves cardio and Latin-inspired dance.Zumba is a group exercise option that combines salsa and aerobics. An excellent workout, Zumba has many benefits, from building endurance to decreasing pain.

Yoga

Yoga is a practice that connects the body, breath, and mind. It involves physical poses, breathing exercises, and meditation.
